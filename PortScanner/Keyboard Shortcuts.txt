Keyboard Shortcuts

Data on key codes can be found at keycode.info

Hit the following keys three times and get a pop up with the corresponding message.

1 SCAN FAIL
2 SCAN ERROR
3 SCAN COMPLETE
4 SCAN RUNNING
5 STEALTH SCAN ACTIVE
6 PING SCAN ACTIVE 
7 TCP SCAN ACTIVE
8 UDP SCAN ACTIVE
9 PORT LOCKED
0 PORT UNLOCKED
