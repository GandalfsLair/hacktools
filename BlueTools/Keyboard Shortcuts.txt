Keyboard Shortcuts

Data on key codes can be found at keycode.info

Hit the following keys three times and get a pop up with the corresponding message.

1 DEVICE - Scan
2 SCAN - Success
3 WARNING - Error
4 DEVICE - Located
5 DEVICE - Upload
6 DEVICE - Download
7 DEVICE - Force Pair
8 DEVICE - Breach
9 DEVICE - Root
0 SCAN - Fail
