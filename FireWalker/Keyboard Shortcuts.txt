Keyboard Shortcuts

Data on key codes can be found at keycode.info

Hit the following keys three times and get a pop up with the corresponding message.

1 FIREWALKER - Enabled
2 FIREWALKER - Error
3 PROBE - Running
4 PROBE - Fail
5 FIREWALL - File Found
6 FIREWALKER - Download
7 FIREWALKER - Upload
8 FIREWALL - Running
9 FIREWALL - Disabled
0 FIREWALL - Bypassed
