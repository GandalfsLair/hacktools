Keyboard Shortcuts

Data on key codes can be found at keycode.info

Hit the following keys three times and get a pop up with the corresponding message.

1 PROBE RUNNING
2 PROBE FAIL
3 PROBE ERROR
4 PROBE PASS
5 ANTIVIRUS? PRESENT
6 ANTIVIRUS? NOT PRESENT
7 FIREWALL? PRESENT
8 FIREWALL? NOT PRESENT
9 PASSWORD? PRESENT
0 PASSWORD? NOT PRESENT
