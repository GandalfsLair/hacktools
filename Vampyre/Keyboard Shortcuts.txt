Keyboard Shortcuts

Data on key codes can be found at keycode.info

Hit the following keys three times and get a pop up with the corresponding message.

1 SCANNING FOR MOBILE TOWERS
2 TOWER DETECTED
3 TOWER OUT OF RANGE
4 TOWER BREACHED
5 SCANNING FOR CELLS
6 CELLS LOCATED
7 ERROR
8 ACCESSING CELL
9 UID LOCATED
0 BRIDGE COMPILED
