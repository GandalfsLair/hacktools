Keyboard Shortcuts

Data on key codes can be found at keycode.info

Hit the following keys three times and get a pop up with the corresponding message.

1 Device - SCANNING
2 Device Scan - PASS
3 Device Scan - FAIL
4 Device - BREACHING
5 Device Breach - PASS
6 Device Breach - FAIL
7 Memory - UPLOAD
8 Memory - DOWNLOAD
9 Force Pair - FAIL
0 Force Pair - PASS
